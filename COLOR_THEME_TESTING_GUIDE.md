## 🚀 How to Test

### Step 1: Hard Refresh Your Browser
- **Mac**: `Cmd + Shift + R`
- **Windows**: `Ctrl + Shift + F5`

### Step 2: Look for the Color Theme Switcher
You'll see a **floating button in the bottom-right corner** of the screen that shows:
- A colored circle (current theme color)
- The current theme name (e.g., "Pure White")
- A dropdown arrow

### Step 3: Click to Open the Theme Selector
Click the button to reveal a panel with all 5 color options:

1. **Pure White** - Timeless, professional, innovative
2. **Emerald Green** - Sustainable, transformative, growth-oriented
3. **Champagne Gold** - Premium, aspirational, excellent
4. **Platinum Silver** - Futuristic, sleek, high-tech
5. **Ivory** - Elegant, calm, refined

### Step 4: Click Any Color to Apply It
- The theme changes **instantly** across the entire site
- Your choice is **automatically saved** to localStorage
- The page will remember your choice on refresh

### Step 5: Test Each Color Thoroughly
For each color, check:
- ✅ **Logo** ("Innvt" text in hero section)

## 🎯 Decision Framework

### Choose **White** if:
- ✅ You want maximum professionalism
- ✅ You want to align with leading innovation brands
- ✅ You want timeless, never-outdated design
- ✅ You prioritize clarity and accessibility
- ✅ You want versatility across all contexts

### Choose **Emerald Green** if:
- ✅ Sustainability is core to your brand (not just a value)
- ✅ You want to stand out from typical tech brands
- ✅ You want warmth and optimism
- ✅ Environmental transformation is central
- ✅ You want a memorable, distinctive identity

### Choose **Gold** if:
- ✅ You want premium, aspirational positioning
- ✅ You love the "reaching for the stars" symbolism
- ✅ You want warmth without environmental associations
- ✅ You want a luxurious, excellent feel
- ✅ You're targeting high-end/enterprise clients

### Choose **Platinum** if:
- ✅ You want a futuristic, space-age aesthetic
- ✅ You prefer subtle sophistication over boldness
- ✅ You want a metallic, high-tech feel
- ✅ You emphasize precision and advanced technology
- ✅ You want something between white and gold

### Choose **Ivory** if:
- ✅ You want softer, more approachable than pure white
- ✅ You prefer elegant refinement
- ✅ You want a slightly warmer feel
- ✅ You want timeless sophistication with character
- ✅ You want to be welcoming yet professional

## 💡 Testing Tips

### 1. Test at Different Times
- Test in daylight and at night
- Different lighting affects color perception
- What looks good now might feel different later

### 2. Test on Different Devices
- Desktop vs mobile
- Different screen calibrations
- Retina vs standard displays

### 3. Test All Pages
- Home page (hero, phase cards)
- Genesis page
- Cultivation page
- Symbiosis page
- Horizon page

### 4. Test Interactions
- Hover over phase cards
- Click links
- Scroll through the page
- Watch how colors interact with quantum field

### 5. Get Second Opinions
- Show to colleagues/friends
- Ask: "What does this color make you feel?"
- Get feedback on brand alignment

### 6. Trust Your Gut
- Your first impression matters
- If it doesn't feel right, it probably isn't
- The right color will feel obvious

### How It Works:
1. **CSS Variables**: All colors use CSS custom properties (`--accent-default`, etc.)
